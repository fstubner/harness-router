# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of
conduct.

The full text is maintained at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/code_of_conduct.md

In short: contributors and maintainers commit to making participation in
this project a friendly, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of background or identity. We expect respectful, collaborative behaviour in
all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other
forum where the project is represented.

## Reporting

Instances of behaviour that violate the Contributor Covenant may be reported
to the project maintainer at **felix.stubner@gmail.com**. All reports will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is
obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement

Maintainers follow the Community Impact Guidelines (Section 4 of the linked
Contributor Covenant) when determining consequences. Actions range from a
private written notice for a first-time minor issue, up to a permanent ban
for sustained or severe violations.
